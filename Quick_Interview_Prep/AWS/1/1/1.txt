## Step-by-Step from the Image

1. **Application** — Your Spring Boot app
2. **Dockerfile** — Used to containerize the application into a Docker Image
3. **Docker Image** — The packaged container image of your app
4. **Push** — The Docker image is pushed to **Amazon ECR** (like a private Docker Hub for AWS)
5. **Elastic Container Registry (ECR)** — Stores your Docker images securely
6. **Run** — ECS pulls the image from ECR and runs it
7. **AWS ECS** — Hosts and manages your running containers
