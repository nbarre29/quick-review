## **What is `__consumer_offsets`?**

`__consumer_offsets` is a **special internal Kafka topic** that stores **consumer group offset information**. It's one of Kafka's most important internal topics.

## **What Does It Store?**

This topic tracks:
- **Which offset each consumer group has read** from each partition
- **Consumer group metadata** (members, assignments, etc.)
- **Committed offsets** - the last successfully processed message position



## **Why Does It Exist?**

### **Before `__consumer_offsets` (Old Kafka):**
- Consumer offsets were stored in **ZooKeeper**
- This was slow and didn't scale well
- ZooKeeper wasn't designed for high-write workloads

### **After `__consumer_offsets` (Kafka 0.9+):**
- Offsets stored in a **Kafka topic** (uses Kafka's own strengths!)
- Much faster and more scalable
- Reduces dependency on ZooKeeper
